About Kill The King

Kill The King is an intense battle game where you must use your wit and reflexes to overthrow the cruel King of Trost. By mastering different skills and upgrading your character, you'll work your way through challenging stages until the final confrontation.

Each battle tests your abilities in a different way, requiring strategy and quick thinking to progress. Collect experience points and gold to enhance your character's attributes and unlock powerful new abilities.

Game Features:

  • Multiple challenging stages with increasing difficulty
  • Character progression system with upgradeable skills
  • Real-time combat requiring quick reflexes
  • Strategic resource management
  • Epic boss battles including the final showdown with the King
  • Track your progress with experience points and achievements

How to Play

Controls

Use the space bar to attack during combat. Timing is crucial - press at the right moment to deliver maximum damage to your opponents.

Character Development

Spend your earned XP to increase your strength, intelligence, and speed. These stats will determine your effectiveness in battle.

Skills & Upgrades

Unlock new abilities and upgrade existing ones to become more powerful. Balance your build to match your playstyle.
